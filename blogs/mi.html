Who is the owner of mi?

At first I want to say that Redmi is not a mobile company, it is a smartphone series by Chinese company Xiaomi. They have 2 smartphone series ——
1. MI series, which is flagship series, the handsets of MI series comes with premium features, better build quality, larger storage, powerful SOCs, good enough cameras.
2. Redmi series, which is a both budget and midrange smartphone series, the handsets come with bigger batteries, good features, good cameras, budget section SOCs(like Snapdragon 400 & 600 series). And the Redmi series smartphones are great in budget section except their MIUI, which comes in both Mi and Redmi, I personally don't like their heavy UI.
Now coming to your question. Lei Jun is the owner, founder and CEO of Xiaomi brand. He is a Chinese entrepreneur also.
