If you thought Shirdi is known only for its temple and places related to the life of Sai Baba then think again. Situated at a walking distance of 10 minutes from Sai Baba temple is the famous Shirdi Water park known as Wet N Joy, the one of a kind amusement theme park started by Malpani group. For tourists visiting Shirdi to seek blessings of Sai Baba, the Shirdi Water park provides welcome change from the tour of religious places of Shirdi. A place for fun and frolic, this amusement theme park is the perfect place for families and friends to enjoy some fun moments amidst the water games and thrilling rides of Wet N Joy. This amazing water park in Shirdi is a favorite among the kids due to the various number of entertainment options available for the tiny tots.
Shirdi water park offers umpteen number of attractions for young and old alike with its thrilling rides, rain dance, kids pool, wave pool, beautiful gardens, fountains along with mouth watering food served at the restaurants. With a nominal entrance fee, a trip to Wet N Joy is worth experiencing by everyone visiting Shirdi. Shirdi water park offers a number of entertainment options for people visiting this place with its wet and dry rides. One can either enjoy the thrilling rides such as Black hole, Anaconda, cyclone and Match 3 or just enjoy the waves at the Thunder wave pool. The Kids planet offers family ride for the entire family and a Kids pool exclusively for kids. One can also showcase their dancing skills on the wooden dance floor while drenching in the artificial rain or enjoy dry rides like Free fall, Jumping frog and Swinging chair. A trip to Shirdi water park is incomplete without tasting the amazing food served at the various restaurants here.
With its various sightseeing options, Shirdi is definitely a major tourist destination. Apart from the various religious tourist spots, Shirdi has other attractions and Shirdi water park is one among them. With its various rides and tasty food, a trip to Wet N Joy is a must for everyone. A famous tourist attraction among families, friends and especially kids, Wet N Joy is a must visit tourist spot in the land of ‘Child of God’.
